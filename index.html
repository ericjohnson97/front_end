About Us

Cloud based. Crowd friendly. Wallet conscious.

At WeTrain, we come to work every day to solve the biggest problem that organizations using deep learning face: Annotating Datasets. In 2017, our founders had to train a neural network to detect objects in real time. Due to the immense cost of having this done by a professional, they were forced to utilize clunky, local software to annotate 5000 images on their own. We couldn’t help but think “There has to be a better way.”

Enter WeTrain, a company designed to streamline the world of deep learning, end to end. Our simple, yet powerful annotation tool means anyone can annotate with ease, and our dynamic database generation tool enables us to generate image databases of nearly any size.
